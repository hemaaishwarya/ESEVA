PROCEDURE FOR UNEMPLOYMENT CERTIFICATE


ELIGIBILITY 

a)         A candidate must pass 10th and must be between the age of 17 to 40 years.               

b)         The annual family income of candidates must be less than Rs12000/-

c)         Candidate should not be a regular student and he must be unemployed.

d)        Candidates avail unemployment allowance according to as follow:

For Blind, Deaf & Dumb impaired candidates:

             After first quarter from the date of   registration.

For Orthopedically Handicapped  candidates:

             After one year from the date of registration.

For Other category candidate:

             After 3 years from the date of  registration

How to apply 

1. An educated unemployed applicant may give an  application for the grant of unemployment allowance to the officer incharge of the employment exchange, where he is registered.
2. A duly completed application form may be sent by post or delivered  personally at the employment exchange against a receipt.
3.
